# NovelNest

Hey Fellows, This is Hdarks, Intorducing you my NovelNest project.

This NovelNest, as the name suggests is the nest of the Web Novels you like.
In this you can be an Author that takes his/her readers into a journey full of Action, Drama, Fantasy, Adventure etc.. Depending on your imagination.
OR you can be a Reader/User that likes to go on these jourines.

For the Authors:
Here you can write your novels, its chapters, add cover images and look after your progress, you can also read reviews given bby the readers, and evaluate your progress.

For Readers/Users:
Here you can Explore various Authors, read novels, write reivews, rate the novels, check your reading progress etc.



The novels in the database are taken from the novelbin.me website, you can check those novels to get to the reading.
